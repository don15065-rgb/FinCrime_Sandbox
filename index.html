About / Disclaimer

FinCrime Simulator is an educational project only. It is not a real AML system, not legal advice, not compliance advice, and not a replacement for institutional policy, law, regulatory guidance, or professional judgement.

All cases, names, transactions, customer profiles, and examples are fictional. The purpose is to help aspiring AML/KYC analysts understand how financial crime concepts may appear in operational work.

Portfolio note: Built as a practical training project to demonstrate AML/KYC knowledge, SQL awareness, product thinking, and front-end development ability.
